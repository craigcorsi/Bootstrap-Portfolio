Hi! Welcome to my new page, powered by Bootstrap. Feel free to browse my portfolio, or contact me. (I'll try to be... responsive!)

I'm a web developer with a background in mathematics, particularly linear algebra, symmetry, and combinatorics. These topics converge in the subject of representation theory, a modern tool with applications in chemistry, signal processing, and cryptography!

I enjoy both the analytic, computational aspects of back-end development and the visual, stylistic aspects of front-end development. This has been a fulfilling boot camp so far, and I look forward to future projects!

Outside of school and work, I really enjoy watching improv comedy. I've also had the pleasure of performing with improv groups such as "Sixplex," "Mouse Town," "Myths and Legends," and "Trondheim is Burning." HUGE Theater is the long-form improv theater I call home.
