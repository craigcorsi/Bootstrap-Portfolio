Electronic Music Game

I love electronic music. Ever since I started playing Dance Dance Revolution in middle school, I've been hooked by the synthetic sounds and repetitive rhythms of electronic music, in all of its forms.

One goal of mine is to design a web-based music/rhythm game where users hit falling notes to the beat of a track, similar to Flash Flash Revolution or Bemuse.

Matrix Machine

While linear equation systems rarely describe reality, they approximate it fairly well. And linear systems have a lot of elegance from a geometric, combinatorial, and algebraic point of view.

I would like to design an online matrix calculator which uses JavaScript objects to store information about matrices, such as the determinant, the rank, and the reduced row echelon form.

Predictive Joke Generator

What has resonated with me the most from improv classes is the philosophy of accepting the given premise and rolling with the punches. If you walk out on stage thinking you're a badger and your scene partner tells you you're in space, then it is most rewarding to be a badger in space!

One project idea I have is to write a program that automatically generates a joke from a given premise. I will have to study how other predictive text generators work -- hopefully the jokes are funny for the right reasons!

Online Creative Writing Journal

I think a minimalistic online journal would be a great platform for creative writers, the philosophy being "put the organizational tools aside and just write your draft."

PixelDraw online drawing program

The other idea I had was for a browser-based program for drawing small 50px by 50px avatars one pixel at a time, similar to the Lite Brite ™.
